In this pane, you can see the location of interesting sites like art galleries, museums, fire stations, career centers and houses in rent near NYU.

You can choose what sites class to want see. When the button is on, you will see the places on the map.

Career Centers and Fire stations is closed to a maximum distance of 10 km and Art galleries an museums is within a range of 5 km.

In this pane, you can choose the option that you want between: Cheapest option, Closest option and Safest option. When there are results, these are optimized to help you in your decision. When you choose "None option", you will see all the options in results pane.

In this pane, you can see all the available houses in rent in a range of 10 km. If you click in each option, you will see details of the house, like: cost, address, distance to NYU and safe indicator.

The safe indicator represents a level of safety around the house in a radius of 200 meters. The less indicator the more safety.

In this pane, you can choose the chart that you want to see between Median rental price and Average Temperature.
Mediant rental price chart shows you the average rent price over the last year. This will help you to make a projection according to the time in which you want to travel.

Average Temperature shows you the maximum (red line), average (blue line) and minimum (green line) temperature over the last year. This will help you to make a projection according to the time in which you want to travel.
