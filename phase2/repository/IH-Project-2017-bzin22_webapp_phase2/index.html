  What Are We?

Having trouble finding parking spots in the big city? Fear not! Save time and energy with Parking Bot! With this app, you can select your current location and be able to find nearby parking spaces that have unused parking meters on them! That way, you'll never have to waste gas, time and energy looking for a place to park. Instead, you can just head straight to your desired location, hassle free!
